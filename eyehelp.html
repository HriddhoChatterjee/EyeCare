Eye Fitness Exercises

1. Focus Shifting

Steps:

  1. Hold your thumb a few inches from your eye.
  2. Focus on it for 5 seconds.
  3. Shift your focus to an object 20 feet away.
  4. Repeat 10 times.
Improves accommodation and flexibility.

2. The Figure Eight

Steps:

  1. Imagine a large number '8' on a wall 10 feet away.
  2. Trace the path with your eyes slowly.
  3. Switch directions after 30 seconds.
Strengthens eye muscles.

3. Palming

Steps:

  1. Rub your palms until they feel warm.
  2. Cup them over your closed eyes (don't press).
  3. Breathe deeply in total darkness for 1 minute.
Relaxes the optic nerve and relieves dry eyes.

The Eye Health Diet

Beta-Carotene

Carrots, Apricots, and Pumpkins. Essential for night vision and preventing cataracts.

Omega-3 Fatty Acids

Fish, Walnuts, and Chia seeds. Great for preventing dry eye syndrome.

Vitamin C & E

Citrus fruits and Nuts. Helps repair damaged eye tissue and reduces age-related decline.
